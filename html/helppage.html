How to use the Atlas

Menu

Home button: go to the landing page, where images, videos and information about light pollution are available.
Single view button: the atlas shows one map of Europa, where information about light pollution and electricity consumption, energy price, gross national product and population density for years 1996, 2000, 2004 and 2011 can be found.
Dual view button: split the browser's window in two sub windows to compare two different information.
Overlay view button: merge the two visualisations from the dual view in one map.
Graph button: correlation between data
Information button: information about the correlation
