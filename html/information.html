Information about the Atlas



The main goal of the light pollution atlas is to show the spatial distribution of light pollution in Europe and gives more information about indicators who could correlate with the light pollution. This can be a motivation for saving more electric energy on an other reason then producing less Co2 emissions. You can have just an overview look on the different topics on predefined maps but you can get also further information, like mathematical correlation between light pollution and a topic of your choice. So dive in the very interesting topic and find out reasons for this bad reviewed phenomenon light pollution.
